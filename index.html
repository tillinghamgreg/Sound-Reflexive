Sound Reflexive

Long-form recordings of real places, offered as environments to spend time in through listening.

No sign-up required at this stage. If you see a prompt to open the SoundCloud app, you can ignore it and listen in your browser. A Bluetooth speaker works particularly well, but phone speakers and headphones are fine too.

Listening examples

Basket Weaving — Winter Afternoon

5 hours • Indoor • Human activity • Still

Quiet basket weaving at home on a wet winter’s day, with distant weather and occasional passing activity.

Kent Weald — Through the Night

7 hours • Outdoor • Night environment • Varied

An overnight recording in the Kent Weald, moving from evening chorus into night, with periods of stillness, marsh frogs, and a nightingale through the dark hours. A helicopter passes directly overhead and trains cut through the distance before the return of birds at dawn.

Listen

Camber Sands — Late Summer

2 hours 45 minutes • Outdoor • Coastal • Busy

A warm, crowded day at Camber Sands at the end of summer, walking along the waterline from Jury’s Gap towards the harbour and back. Children move through the shallows and along the shore, with games, finds, and voices carried on the air, while further back the dunes hold their own rhythm.

Listen

Dungeness — Marsh and Sound Mirrors

12.5 hours • Outdoor • Mixed environment • Varied

A long recording at the sound mirrors on the Dungeness reserve, where birds, marsh frogs, distant machinery, aircraft, gunfire, and the railway move in and out of the landscape. Periods of activity give way to quieter hours of wind and space.
